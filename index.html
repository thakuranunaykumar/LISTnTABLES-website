INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE - 2023

The Indian Premier League, also known as IPL 16 or for sponsorship reasons, TATA IPL 2023, was the fifteenth season of the Indian Premier League (IPL), a professional Twenty20 cricket league established by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007. The tournament was played from 31 March 2022 to 29 May 2022.
